### 题目
用凑微分法求不定积分
$$\int\cfrac{1}{x(\ln^2x-1)}\mathrm{d}x$$
### 解
$\cfrac{1}{x}$和$\mathrm{d}x$可以凑成$\mathrm{d}\ln x$，于是
$$\int\cfrac{1}{x(\ln^2x-1)}\mathrm{d}x=\int\cfrac{1}{ln^2x-1}\mathrm{d}\ln x=\cfrac{1}{2}\ln\left|\cfrac{\ln x-1}{\ln x+1}\right|+C$$
