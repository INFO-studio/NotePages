1. N-COUNT 可数名词
	- 功绩；伟业；壮举
	- (approval) If you refer to an action, or the result of an action, as a **feat**, you admire it because it is an impressive and difficult achievement.
	- *A racing car is an extraordinary **feat** of engineering.*
		- *赛车是工程学上一项相当了不起的成就。*
