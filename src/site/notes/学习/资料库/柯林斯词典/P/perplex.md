1. V-T 及物动词
	- 使困惑；使担心；使迷惑
	- If something **perplexes** you, it confuses and worries you because you do not understand it or because it causes you difficulty.
	- *It **perplexed** him because he was tackling it the wrong way.*
		- *由于处理不当， 这个问题令他大伤脑筋。*
