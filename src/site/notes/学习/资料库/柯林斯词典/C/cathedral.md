1. N-COUNT 可数名词
	- 大教堂；主教座堂
	- A **cathedral** is a very large and important church which has a bishop in charge of it.
	- *...St. Paul's Cathedral.*
		- *……圣保罗大教堂。*
	- *...the cathedral city of Canterbury.*
		- *……有主教座堂的坎特伯雷市。*
