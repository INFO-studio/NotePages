1. N-UNCOUNT 不可数名词
	- 诱惑力；吸引力；魅力
	- The **allure** of something or someone is the pleasing or exciting quality that they have.
	- *It's a game that has really lost its **allure**.*
		- *这是一项的确已失去了魅力的游戏。*
	- *...the captivating **allure** of Isabelle Adjani.*
		- *……伊莎贝拉·阿佳妮令人倾倒的魅力。*
