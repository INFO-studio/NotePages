1. N-MASS 物质名词
	- 合金
	- An **alloy** is a metal that is made by mixing two or more types of metal together.
	- *Bronze is an **alloy** of copper and tin.*
		- *青铜是铜和锡的合金。*
	- *The company produces titanium **alloy**.*
		- *该公司生产钛合金。*
