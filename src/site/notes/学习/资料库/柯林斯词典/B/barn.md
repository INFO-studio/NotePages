1. N-COUNT 可数名词
	- 谷仓，牲口房
	- A **barn** is a building on a farm in which animals, animal food, or crops can be kept.

2. N-COUNT 可数名词
	- 大仓棚
	- A **barn** is any large building, especially an unattractive one

3. N-COUNT 可数名词
	- 靶，核反应截面单位
	- a unit of nuclear cross section equal to $10^{28}$ square meter.
